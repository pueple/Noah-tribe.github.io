错位感知

Dislocation perception

作品介绍

Introduction 

感知从何而来？这是由于神经系统的功能分区让我们的感官与知觉产生了一一对应的关系。若是这样解释人类五感，即可分析为：眼睛对应视觉捕捉图像；耳朵对应听觉捕捉声音；鼻子对应嗅觉捕捉气味；舌头对应味觉捕捉味道；躯体对应触感捕捉肉体感受。一般情况下这样的对应关系具有其稳定性和不可拆分性，这一特性是由人类大脑构造与其固定的功能分区决定的。

Where does perception come from? This is due to the functional division of the nervous system so that our senses and perception have a one-to-one correspondence. If we explain the five senses in this way, we can analyze them as follows: eyes correspond to vision to capture images; ears to hearing to capture sounds; nose to smell to capture smell; tongue to taste to capture taste; body to touch to capture physical feelings. Generally, such correspondence has its stability and indivisibility, which is determined by the structure of human brain and its fixed functional regions.

这样的对应关系是否真的一成不变？若是将这样的固定关系错位，我们将会感知到怎样的世界？是否能够像奇幻电影中一样拥有超感知觉？

Is this correspondence really unchangeable? If such fixed relationship is misplaced, what kind of world will we perceive? Is it possible to have super sensory perception as in fantasy movies?
